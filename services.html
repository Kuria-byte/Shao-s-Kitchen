Frequently asked questions

Hi there! Shao's Kitchen always strives to provide a hassle-free cake purchase experience - fewer clicks to get your cake fix or to put a smile on someone's face through us!

How do I place my order?

Reach out to me via social media or phone and place your order directly. To ensure quality and preparation place your order within a 24+ hours notice.Finally a 50% deposit will be charged and the rest will be cleared upoon delivery .

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! Delivery can be arranged conviniently within Nairobi CBD. For areas outside this we charge a minimum delivery fee

Should I refrigerate my cake upon delivery?

Totally! The cakes need to be refrigerated upon delivery to keep the cake fresh and prevent ruining the treat..

Can I add special baking instructions for the baker?

We can't guarantee that all special requests can be fulfilled, with the limited time period and available materials taken into consideration, but we will definitely try our best! Do let us know about your requests at the cart page or drop us a Whatsapp text on our hotline..
