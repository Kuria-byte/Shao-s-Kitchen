Why Choose us?

Exuding style, sophistication and individuality Shao's Kitchen specialises in custom made, handcrafted birthday cakes and special occasion cakes that look and taste heavenly.

Cooking and Baking is love made visible

When you engage with your family for a meal, it is our mission to engage your body and your senses. Above and beyond the spectacular taste, we hope you relish in the beauty of a product that only dedicated human hands can create. We love artisan cakes. Every extra step we take in the creation of our products is an effort to make you love them just as much as we do.

Our Philosophy

We believe that food should not only nourish, but also inspire, so we create all of our products with that philosophy in mind.

  • We use our hands to shape and form every cake.
  • We use only the highest quality ingredients.
  • The aroma and flavor should be pure.
  • The texture and the chew should be right for the use.
  • We strive for the most beautiful finish.
